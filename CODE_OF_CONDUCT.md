Code of Conduct
Our Commitment

We are committed to creating an open, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone who participates in this project.

All contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to uphold this Code of Conduct in all project spaces, including code repositories, documentation, issue trackers, discussions, and external communication channels related to the project.

Our goal is to foster a community built on professionalism, trust, and constructive collaboration.

Expected Behavior

By participating in this project, you agree to:

Be respectful and considerate in communication

Assume good intent while staying open to feedback

Give and receive constructive criticism gracefully

Focus on what is best for the project and the community

Use welcoming and inclusive language

Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct of any kind

Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory language

Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith arguments

Public or private harassment

Publishing othersâ€™ private information without permission

Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional or unsafe in a workplace environment

Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

All project spaces (repositories, documentation, issue trackers, etc.)

Public spaces when representing the project or its community

Private communication if it impacts the safety or well-being of a community member

Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers.

When reporting, include:

A description of the incident

Any relevant links, screenshots, or evidence

Your contact information (optional, but helpful for follow-up)

All reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed in good faith.

Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may take any action deemed appropriate, including:

Warning the individual

Removing content

Temporary or permanent ban from the project

Decisions will be made with the goal of protecting the community and maintaining a safe, professional environment.

Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by widely adopted open-source community standards and has been adapted to fit the needs and values of this project.

By contributing to this project, you agree to follow these guidelines and help maintain a respectful and productive community.
